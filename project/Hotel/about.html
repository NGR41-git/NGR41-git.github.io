About Mahdan Hotel Tabriz

The five-star Mahdan Hotel in Tabriz was put into operation in Nowruz 1401 on the roof of the beautiful city of Tabriz. The main tower of the hotel with a beautiful glass facade is built on 17 floors and has 178 modern rooms and suites equipped with convenient amenities. While this complex is far from the hustle and bustle of the city and has a peaceful and pleasant environment, it is not far from shopping centers and tourist attractions such as El Goli Park and Zoo, Sortmeh and Helia Water Park. Eel Goli Tabriz Hotel wishes you a memorable stay with special facilities and services with experienced staff in a pleasant atmosphere.
